1. What Are Affiliate Links?

1.1 Understanding Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based advertising model where businesses (advertisers) reward affiliates (publishers like RPM AI) for driving traffic or sales to their websites through affiliate links. These links contain tracking codes that identify RPM AI as the referring source when you make a purchase.

1.2 How Affiliate Links Work

When you click on an affiliate link on our website:

  1. A tracking cookie is placed on your device (with your consent, as per our Cookie Policy)
  2. This cookie identifies RPM AI as the referral source
  3. If you make a purchase within the cookie duration (typically 30-90 days), we earn a commission
  4. The commission comes from the advertiser's marketing budget, not from your purchase price

Key Characteristics of Affiliate Links

  • Transparent Tracking: Links contain tracking parameters visible in the URL
  • No Price Increase: You pay exactly the same price as direct purchasers
  • Limited Duration: Cookies typically last 30-90 days
  • Performance-Based: We only earn when you complete a qualified action

3. No Extra Cost to You

3.1 Price Parity Guarantee

One of the most important aspects of our affiliate disclosure is confirming that you do NOT pay any additional costs when purchasing through our affiliate links. The price you pay is exactly the same as if you went directly to the merchant's website.

3.2 How This Works

Affiliate commissions are funded from the merchant's marketing budget, not added to the consumer price. Here's the breakdown:

Cost Structure Comparison

Direct Purchase: Product Price = Production Cost + Merchant Profit + Marketing Budget

Affiliate Purchase: Product Price = Production Cost + Merchant Profit + (Marketing Budget - Commission)

The commission comes from the marketing budget allocation, not from an increased product price.
