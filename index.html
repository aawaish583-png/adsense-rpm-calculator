Complete Guide to AdSense RPM Recovery

What is AdSense RPM and Why Yours Might Be Low

RPM (Revenue Per Mille) represents how much you earn per 1,000 pageviews. The average RPM varies by niche and geography:

  • Finance/Business: $18-25 RPM (USA)
  • Technology/SaaS: $14-20 RPM (USA)
  • Health/Wellness: $16-22 RPM (USA)
  • Lifestyle: $8-12 RPM (USA)

If your RPM is 30%+ below these benchmarks, technical issues are likely costing you money.

How Website Speed Directly Impacts AdSense Earnings

Google's ad auction system favors fast-loading websites with:

  • Higher-quality ad placements
  • Better viewability scores
  • Premium advertiser competition
  • Higher click-through rates

Research shows each 1-second delay reduces RPM by 5-8%.
